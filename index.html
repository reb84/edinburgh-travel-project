My favorite places in Edinburgh

Edinburgh Castle

Visit one of Scotland's most popular attractions and experience the story of the Defender of the Nation.

Edinburgh Castle is alive with exciting tales of its time as a military fortress, royal residence and prison of war. When you climb Castle Hill, you will walk in the footsteps of soldiers, kings and queens – and even the odd pirate or two.

Learn more

Frankenstein Pub

Welcome to Frankenstein Pub where Mary Shelley’s tale gets real!

Housed in a 19th century church, Frankenstein is a unique themed bar on George IV Bridge, the heart of Edinburgh's Old Town, just a stone's throw away from the popular tourist destinations along the Royal Mile.
